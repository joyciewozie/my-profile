Programming | UX | Customers' needs

Joyce

My web development journey started from answering repetitive customers’ questions about an app: At my first company, I bore the brunt of customers’ frustrations as they struggled to navigate the company’s app. Through my interactions with customers, I became attuned to their pain points. But realising that it is not scalable to personally assist every customer, I learnt that an app’s user interface is essential for creating a smooth customer journey.

From there, I delved into user experience (UX) by designing new features with Product teams and taking UX classes. While creating prototypes, I discovered an interest to bring my concepts to life.

I signed up for Le Wagon’s coding bootcamp in Jan’23, where I have been learning to build web applications. After the bootcamp, I am looking to pursue a tech role where I can create seamless web experiences by combining my programming skills, UX knowledge and acumen of customers’ needs.
